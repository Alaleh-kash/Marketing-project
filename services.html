Setup Hosting & Domain

Setting up hosting and a domain for a website is a crucial step in establishing an online presence. Hosting refers to the space on a server where your website’s files, data, and content will be stored, while a domain is the unique web address that visitors will use to access your site.

SEO

Google Business Registration

Google Business Registration is the process by which businesses claim and verify their online presence on Google’s various platforms, including Google Maps and Google Search. It allows businesses to create and manage their Google My Business (GMB) listing, which serves as an online business profile containing essential information like the business’s name, address, phone number, website, hours of operation, and customer reviews.
